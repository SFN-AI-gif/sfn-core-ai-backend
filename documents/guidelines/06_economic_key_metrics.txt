Title: Economic Key Metrics
Category: Facility Management
Priority: 1

Economic performance indicators are crucial for managing and evaluating racquet facilities. Key topics include:

- Court utilization and scheduling  
- Pricing strategy  
- Revenue per court  
- ROI (Return on Investment)  
- Ancillary revenue (food, retail, etc.)

These metrics guide financial decisions and sustainability, especially in multi-sport or hybrid business models.
